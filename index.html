About Me

I’m a UI/UX Designer and Frontend Developer with over 2 years of design experience and 1+ year in frontend development. I specialize in building responsive, accessible websites and applications across industries such as real estate, e-commerce, law firm, social security and community platforms. I’ve successfully designed and shipped more than 6 production-ready projects, with a strong focus on performance, user-centered design, data-driven decision-making, and scalable, maintainable component systems.

I specialize in both Low-Fidelity and High-Fidelity design, using tools like Sketch, Figma, and Adobe XD, alongside frontend technologies such as JavaScript, HTML, CSS, and modern styling frameworks. I excel at collaborating with cross-functional teams, turning business objectives into scalable technical solutions, and continuously improving products through data-driven insights, analytics, and experimentation.

My technical expertise extends to state management backend integration with REST APIs, and tools like Git/GitHub, Vercel. I'm also skilled in microsoft office equipments like Word, Excel and PowerPoint.
